1. Put the elevator in inspection mode.
2. Make sure that the Lobby lock is in the "Lock" position.
3. Verify that no other service is running on the elevator.
4. Connect the USB cable between the DAD and the elevator controller.
5. Select the firmware package to upload (SBF or ZIP format).
6. Upload the package using the upload section below.
7. Select the specific firmware file from the table.
8. Select the boards you wish to update (or choose "Update All").
9. Start the update process. Do not remove power or USB during update.
10. Wait for all boards to reach 100% completion.
11. Click "Done" to finalize the update and restore normal mode.
